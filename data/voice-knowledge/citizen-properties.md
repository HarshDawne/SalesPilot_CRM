## 1. QUICK MARKET OVERVIEW

**Mumbai:**
- Premium high-demand pockets: South Mumbai, Bandra, Juhu, Andheri, Powai, Chembur
- Fast-moving suburban clusters: Goregaon, Malad, Kandivali, Kharghar
- Inventory: Mix of older buildings, redevelopment projects, luxury new-builds

**Thane:**
- Strong growth corridor, family-focused suburbs
- Key areas: Thane West, Majiwada, Ghodbunder Road, Kalyan
- More mid-range apartments, improving infrastructure, attractive yields

**Key Agent Focus:**
- Emphasize locality (schools, hospitals, transit)
- Clarity on carpet vs built-up area
- Exact possession timeline for under-construction units

## 3. PROPERTY TYPES & CONFIGURATIONS

- **Apartment / Flat:** 1 BHK (450-650 sqft carpet), 2 BHK (650-900 sqft), 3 BHK (900-1400 sqft), 4 BHK+ (1500+ sqft)
- **Builder Floor:** Smaller apartment blocks, single-owner-managed floors
- **Villa / Row House:** Premium, limited supply in select suburbs
- **Office / Retail / Shop:** Commercial units vary by location and footfall
- **Plot / Land:** Mostly in Thane outskirts, Kalyan corridor

**Note:** Always confirm whether area is carpet, built-up, or super-built-up. Use carpet area for value per sqft calculations.

## 6. LEAD QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST

Ask in order:
1. Transaction type: buy / sell / rent / lease
2. Property type: residential / commercial / plot / villa
3. Preferred areas (list top 3)
4. Budget range (min - max)
5. Configuration: 1/2/3 BHK, office size, shop frontage
6. Timeline: ASAP / 0-30 / 30-90 / 90+ days
7. Decision maker: single / joint / NRI / company
8. Financing: loan required? pre-approved? cash?
9. Site visit availability
10. Deal-breakers (floor, pet policy, parking, Vastu, etc.)

## 8. TOP OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

1. **"Just browsing"** → "No problem — can I ask your budget and preferred area so I don't send irrelevant options?"
2. **"Price too high"** → "What price range did you have in mind? I'll show comparable options."
3. **"Need to consult family"** → "Would a short 10-minute call with your family help?"
4. **"Getting a loan"** → "Have you been pre-approved? I can share properties that fit your sanction amount."
5. **"Found cheaper online"** → "Can I check the listing? I'll verify credentials and compare all costs."

## 10. FINANCING & LOAN QUICK GUIDE

- **Common lenders:** Major banks and NBFCs
- **Loan-to-Value:** Typically 75-90% depending on profile
- **Required docs:** ID/Aadhar, PAN, income proof, bank statements, property documents
- **Timelines:** Pre-approval 1-3 days, sanction 7-14 days, disbursal post valuation

## 11. LEGAL & TAX NOTES (INDIA)

- Stamp duty and registration charges vary by state; buyer usually pays
- Capital gains tax applies for sellers (consult CA)
- Rental income is taxable for landlords
- Always recommend verified advocates for complex title issues
